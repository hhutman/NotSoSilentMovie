Twitch Odyssey

A live stream performance, The Sirens

Twitch Odyssey explores the intersection of devised theater, collaborative filmmaking and game-based online media through a contemporary adaptation of Homer’s Odyssey. The content for this performance stream is made by you, the audience/player/content maker. It can be made anonymously, or not. You can work alone or with family or friends.

Odysseus’s journey is at once facilitated by the gods and by his own cunning and resolve. Divine intervention and personal agency keeps him alive along the way and eventually gets him home.

As Odysseus and his crew approach the island of the Sirens, Odysseus, as instructed by Circe, tells the crew to plug their ears with beeswax and has them bind him to the mast of the ship. Such is the power of the lovely Sirens.

In this performance we will explore what is it that each of us longs for, or is drawn to?  What is it that pulls us off course?
As you participate you might consider the following:    What shapes the direction of your day?  What drives you?  What distracts you?  Is it something (desire, longing, lust) from within or something (expectations, accepted behavior, ideas of success, ideas identity) in the culture?  In The Odyssey, we learn that giving in to the Sirens brings certain death. Do you believe that? There is no right answer. Just something to think about.
